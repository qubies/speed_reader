CMPUT 656 Speed Reader

CMPUT 656 AI Assisted Speed Reader

This is an experiment for CMPUT 656, a graduate class in human <-> AI interaction at the University of Alberta. If you want to participate, we appreciate it! Please read the instuctinos below and then generate a username and password by clicking on the button to get started!

I have questions....

What is it?

This is a speed reading application that attemps to increase reader comprehension by highlighting imortant parts of the text for the reader.

What do I have to do?

Not much, just read some text and answer comprehension questions. The text passages are intended to be simple, they are aimed at a grade 4-6 level.

I don't want to look stupid if I get it wrong...

Not a problem, we have no way of knowing who did what. The IDs are randomly generated and the study is double blind. This means that you will either get the AI assistant or be placed in a control group where heuristics assist you.

Instructions

When you generate an account you are presented with a username and password. This allows you to log in and out and finish your trial.Please only create one account

When you log in there will be a start button, click that when you are ready to read. The reader will display words in the center of your screen, one at a time. This allows you to read without moving your eyes.

When you are reading the text, you can increase the speed by pressing the UP arrow and decrease it by pressing the DOWN arrow. If you want to rewind the text press the LEFT arrow and to skip a line press the RIGHT arrow
